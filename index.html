Vicente Matus Icaza

I build and study optical camera communication systems for real-world and unconventional scenarios, combining optics, embedded hardware, signal processing, and experimental prototyping.

Postdoctoral Researcher, IDeTIC — Instituto para el Desarrollo Tecnológico y la Innovación en Comunicaciones
Universidad de Las Palmas de Gran Canaria · Spain

Ph.D. in Business, Internet and Communications Technologies (EmITIC), ULPGC (2021), under a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions fellowship. Electrical Engineer, Universidad de Chile (2018). My work focuses on Optical Camera Communications, Optical Wireless Communications, experimental platforms, embedded systems, and applied prototyping. I am also co-founder of Visionar Automatización SL.
