Vicente Matus Icaza

I develop camera-based optical wireless communication systems that work in real-world conditions — fog, sandstorms, underwater, wearables, satellites — and publish the experimental results so others can build on them.

Ph.D. (2021, ULPGC, EU Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow) · B.Sc. Electrical Engineering (2018, Universidad de Chile). Previously visiting researcher at Instituto de Telecomunicações, Aveiro (2022–2024) and Northumbria University (2020–2021). Co-founder of a technology startup focused on automation, instrumentation and intelligent systems.
