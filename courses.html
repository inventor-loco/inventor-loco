Flagship Seminar

Optical Camera Communications (OCC)

Camera-based links · experimental measurements · signal processing

A seminar centered on the physical and algorithmic foundations of OCC, from camera hardware and rolling/global shutter behavior to measurement campaigns and receiver-side processing.

  • Visible light communication basics, LEDs, and current-source circuits
  • Ground truth, metadata, hallway/outdoor/atmospheric measurements
  • Correlation, matched filtering, Gaussian mixtures, and DSP workflows

Solar Energy Seminar

PV systems · power electronics · installation practice

A practical seminar that starts with photovoltaic fundamentals and grows into system design, safety, power electronics, installation workflow, grid connection, monitoring, and troubleshooting.

  • Cells, panels, wiring, shadowing, protections, and grounding
  • Inverters, PWM, MPPT, batteries, mobile systems, and EV-related topics
  • Legality, paperwork, maintenance, diagnostics, and business insights

Digital Fabrication

3D Printing & Digital Manufacturing

CAD/CAM · machine architecture · troubleshooting

A hands-on pathway through additive manufacturing and small-scale digital fabrication, covering printers, materials, workflows, remote operation, and community/fab-lab culture.

  • Types of machines, filaments, hot ends, mechanics, and electronics
  • 3D modelling basics, parameters, maintenance, and optimization
  • Recycling, online model sourcing, printer farms, and business insight

Home Systems

Pure Water at Home: Reverse Osmosis

Installation · water quality · maintenance

A practical seminar on domestic reverse osmosis systems, from component-level understanding to installation, cleaning, maintenance, measurement, upgrades, and common failure modes.

  • RO stages, tank, post-filtering, pumps, and water-quality monitoring
  • Installation sequence, membrane handling, troubleshooting, and modifications
  • Advanced systems, remote monitoring, recycling, and business considerations
